About Us

At Beyond Canvas, we believe that art can change lives. Founded by three passionate artists — Shradha Mishra, Shraddha Jha, and Ashish Kumar — our goal is to guide students in discovering their creativity and self-expression through art.

Our Philosophy

Art is more than drawing and painting — it’s emotion, imagination, and inspiration. We teach with patience, joy, and dedication, helping every student develop a personal style and deep love for creativity. Each class is designed to make learning art both structured and soulful.

We embrace every student’s uniqueness and focus on progress over perfection. Whether you’re sketching your first line or creating your next masterpiece, Beyond Canvas is where your artistic journey truly begins.
